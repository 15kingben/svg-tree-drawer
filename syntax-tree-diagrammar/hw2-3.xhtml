Chapter 4, Problem 8: Agreement and Case Marking in Wambaya

  1. We should put the CASE feature into the AGR feature structure. By doing so, the Specifier-Head Agreement Constraint will ensure that the verb (head) and the NP (specifier) have the same case. Furthermore, the Head-Feature principle will ensure that the CASE on NP and N will be the same.
  2. Yes. The rules for English's determiner-noun agreement has been defined in terms of the Specifier-Head Agreement Constraint. And this same constraint, with the addition of CASE to AGR, will also serve to define Wambaya's determiner-noun agreement.
    bungmanyani word HEAD noun AGR ① CASE E GEND F VAL COMPS SPR D AGR ①
    ngankiyaga word HEAD det AGR CASE E GEND F VAL COMPS SPR
    bungmaji word HEAD noun AGR ① CASE A GEND M VAL COMPS SPR D AGR ①
    iniyaga word HEAD det AGR CASE A GEND M VAL COMPS SPR
